Frequently asked questions

How does the free trial work?

Your free trial starts on registration and it will last for 30 days. You can cancel your free trial at any time without charge and once the 30 days trial has ended, your paid registration will start. At registration, further ID verification may be required to access the full service which may take up to 5 days.

Are there any conditions to qualify for the free trial?

To qualify for the free trial you must be a new customer to CreditExpert.

How much does CreditExpert cost?

For CreditExpert a monthly fee of £14.99 applies after your free trial.

Is CreditExpert the right product for me?

If you’re just looking for your free Experian Credit Score or identity protection, take a look at what Experian product is right for you here.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, to cancel CreditExpert, just log in to your Experian account and go to ‘My Subscriptions’. Here you can cancel any paid subscriptions you have but keep your free Experian account and find all the information you need about any payments or refunds you might be due. Alternatively, you can contact us.
